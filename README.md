The Silhouette Club Website is the official digital platform developed for Silhouette, Bennett University's creative and fine arts society.

The website was designed and developed to serve as a centralized hub for showcasing the club's activities, artistic initiatives, events, and creative achievements. It provides members and visitors with an immersive experience that reflects the identity and artistic spirit of the society.

A significant emphasis was placed on creating a visually rich and interactive user experience through the incorporation of custom animations, engaging interfaces, and dynamic content presentation. The website features a digital newsletter flipbook system that allows users to browse club publications in an interactive magazine-like format, enhancing accessibility and user engagement. Several newsletters showcased on the platform were personally designed and curated as part of the project's creative contributions.

The platform also serves as a digital gallery that showcases paintings, illustrations, and artworks created by both the developer and fellow club artists, with proper permissions and collaboration. This not only highlights individual artistic talent but also promotes the collective creative culture of the society.

The project demonstrates expertise in front-end web development, UI/UX design, animation integration, digital publishing, content curation, and collaborative creative work. Beyond technical implementation, the website represents an effort to preserve and celebrate the artistic identity of the university community through technology.
