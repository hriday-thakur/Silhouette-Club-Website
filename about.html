Silhouette – The Art Club of Bennett University

Silhouette – The Art Club is a vibrant community of creative minds at Bennett University that celebrates imagination, expression, and innovation through diverse forms of art. The club provides a platform for students to explore their artistic potential, whether through painting, sketching, crafting, digital art, or experimental mediums. Silhouette believes that art is not just about aesthetics but also about storytelling, culture, and emotions that connect people beyond words. The club organizes workshops, competitions, exhibitions, and collaborative projects to nurture talent and foster creativity among students. Events often blend traditional techniques with contemporary trends, encouraging participants to push boundaries and create meaningful works of art. From themed exhibitions to live art sessions, Silhouette is known for its engaging activities that bring together both beginners and experienced artists. Beyond individual growth, Silhouette thrives on teamwork and collaboration, offering members opportunities to learn from each other and showcase their work at university fests and inter-college events. It also emphasizes art as a medium of social change and self-expression, making it both impactful and enjoyable. With its dynamic energy and inclusive spirit, Silhouette continues to inspire students to embrace their creativity and leave a mark through the power of art. Follow their journey on @silhouette_bu
